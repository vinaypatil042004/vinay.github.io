Electronic Components

Resistors

Resistors are passive electrical components that limit or regulate the flow of electrical current in an electronic circuit.

Capacitors

Capacitors are passive components that store electrical energy temporarily in an electric field.

Inductors

Inductors are passive components that store energy in a magnetic field when electrical current flows through them.

Diodes

Diodes are semiconductor devices that allow current to flow in one direction only, acting as a one-way valve for current.

Transistors

Transistors are semiconductor devices used to amplify or switch electronic signals and electrical power.

SCR (Silicon Controlled Rectifier)

SCRs are four-layer semiconductor devices that control high power by acting as a switch, triggered by a gate signal.

SSR (Solid State Relay)

SSRs are electronic switching devices that use semiconductor components to switch loads without mechanical parts.

Microcontroller Boards

Arduino Uno

The Arduino Uno is a popular microcontroller board based on the ATmega328P microcontroller, ideal for beginners and DIY projects.

Raspberry Pi

The Raspberry Pi is a small, affordable computer that can be used for a variety of electronics projects and learning programming.

ESP8266

The ESP8266 is a low-cost Wi-Fi microchip with full TCP/IP stack and microcontroller capability, widely used in IoT applications.

ESP32

The ESP32 is a powerful, low-cost microcontroller with integrated Wi-Fi and Bluetooth, suitable for IoT and embedded applications.
